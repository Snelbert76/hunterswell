The questions I help answer

Should we buy it, upgrade it, replace it, build it ourselves or wait?

Most SME technology decisions start with pressure: a vendor pushing a renewal, an ageing system becoming risky, an AI product being sold as a silver bullet, or a project that is drifting without anyone giving a straight answer.

  • Is this system the right fit for our business?
  • Is the quote fair, complete and commercially sensible?
  • Are we solving the real problem or buying around it?
  • Should we build, buy, integrate or simplify?
  • What will this decision mean in two or three years?
  • Who is marking the vendor’s homework?
