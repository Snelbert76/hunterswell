Why clients involve me

Most technology problems are really business decisions.

SME leaders are often asked to approve systems, upgrades, integrations or AI initiatives without having a truly independent person in the room. Suppliers know their products. Internal teams know their frustrations. But somebody still has to decide what is commercially sensible.

That is where I help: by translating the technical detail into practical business judgement.

  • A vendor is pushing a major upgrade or renewal.
  • A core system is holding the business back.
  • You are considering CRM, ERP, finance, workflow or field-service software.
  • You are unsure whether to build, buy or integrate.
  • AI sounds important, but you do not want to waste money on hype.
  • A project is slipping and you need a straight answer.
