AIVis

Visibility for AI-enabled operations.

AIVis is being developed to give organisations clearer insight into how AI systems, automated workflows and decision-support tools behave after they leave the prototype stage.

Purpose

Move beyond black-box AI.

AIVis is intended to help teams inspect behaviour, identify risks and build confidence in AI-enabled systems.

Status

Early product development.

The product is currently being shaped under Hunters Well, with the website acting as the first public placeholder for the product line.
